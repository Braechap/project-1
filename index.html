Gingerbread House Recipe

*Ingredients for creating the gingerbread cookies include*

  • Molasses and spices for flavor
  • Egg
  • Brown sugar instead of white granulated sugar

Instructions for making the Gingerbread Cookies

  1. Chill the dough for at least 3 hours.
  2. Scoop out half of the prepared dough, wrap it, and flatten it out.
  3. Roll it out with flour underneath using a rolling pin and your own workspace.
  4. Place the cut-out cookies onto a lined baking sheet, 1 inch apart.

Icing the cookies

Remember you always have to ice your gingerbread cookies so they get a face and a personality
